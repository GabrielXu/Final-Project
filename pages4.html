History

Funny Movie Corporation(FMC) was founded in 2008. Zihan Wu, the CEO, chairman, and the founder of FMC was one of the top students in The High School Affiliated to Renmin, whose intented to offer high-quality filmmaking service through his company. "Dare to think, dare to do" roughly expresses the spirit of the company. In 2010, FMC officially started to accept individual services. Nowadays, the satisfaction rate of FMC's services have reached 93%.
